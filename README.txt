Controls:

Move forward or forward diagonal. Rotates if not facing that direction.
qwe
a d
zxc

Shift+move to move w/o rotating

F = open/close door (only in front)

M = melee

Space = cycle through units

Enter = New turn/reset AP


Instructions:
The Space Marines (blue) must go throught the space hulk to eliminate
the Genestealers (red). The genestealers are much stronger in melee, 
but the marines can shoot (not implemented). Units' AP (action points) are displayed
next to their sprite. Dice rolls are shown in the netbeans output log. 

for more info about rules, visit
https://spacehulkcodex.wordpress.com/2017/10/07/space-hulk-revised-rules-update-2-1/
